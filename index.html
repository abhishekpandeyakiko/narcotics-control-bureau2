"The State shall endeavour to bring about prohibition of the consumption, except for medicinal purposes, of intoxicating drugs injurious to health."

Legislative Evolution

Three Central Acts that form the backbone of drug law enforcement.

1940
Drugs & Cosmetics Act

Regulates import, manufacture & distribution.

1985
The NDPS Act

Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act.

1988
PITNDPS Act

Prevention of Illicit Traffic in Narcotic Drugs.

Genesis of NCB

Established 17th March, 1986

Born from the mandate of the NDPS Act, 1985, to serve as the nodal agency for drug law enforcement in India.
